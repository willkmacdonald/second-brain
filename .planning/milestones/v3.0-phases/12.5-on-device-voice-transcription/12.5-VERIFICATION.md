### Human Verification Required

### 1. On-Device Voice Capture End-to-End

**Test:** Open the app with EAS development build on iPhone (iOS 26). Select voice mode. Grant speech recognition permission. Tap record, say "need milk and eggs from Jewel", tap stop.
**Expected:** Words appear on screen in real-time as you speak. Pulsing red ring around record button. After stop, transcribed text stays visible, "Classifying..." appears (no "Uploading..." step). Filed toast appears.
**Why human:** Requires real iOS device with SFSpeechRecognizer on-device support and EAS development build installed.

### 2. Transcription Accuracy (VOICE-OD-02)

**Test:** Record several informal captures: shopping items ("cat litter and paper towels"), reminders ("call the vet about the cat"), quick notes ("check if the new case fits").
**Expected:** Transcription captures words accurately enough for classifier to file correctly. Minor word errors acceptable.
**Why human:** VOICE-OD-02 explicitly defined as manual UAT requirement. Cannot verify transcription accuracy programmatically.

### 3. Cloud Fallback Path

**Test:** On a device where on-device recognition is unavailable (older iOS or simulator), verify the app silently falls back to cloud upload.
**Expected:** Timer shows during recording (not real-time text), audio uploads via sendVoiceCapture, "Uploading..." then "Classifying..." stages appear.
**Why human:** Requires a device without SFSpeechRecognizer on-device support. Cannot simulate capability check programmatically.
