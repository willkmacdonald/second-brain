## Decisions Made
- **Site 1 fix approach:** Used `get_current_span().set_attribute()` in each capture handler (not middleware) because the capture_trace_id is only known after header parsing in the handler body. This retroactively tags the already-started AppRequests span before it ends.
- **Cosmos KQL migration:** Switched the COSMOS_DIAGNOSTIC_LOGS template from `AzureDiagnostics | where activityId_g` to `AppDependencies | where Properties.["capture.trace_id"]` because the spike memo proved activityId_g does not hold x-ms-client-request-id.
